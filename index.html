about

Hi, I'm Andrew. I have a B.S. in mechanical engineering. I am a professional with an EIT and I have experience in data analytics, customer relations, design and manufacturing through jobs and passion projects. This website is to showcase some of the things mentioned on my resume, or otherwise thought to be worth sharing.

I'm seeking to solve problems with companies who have a stake in their end user's well being. Areas like areas like power generation, manufacturing, automation, health products, logistics, and others are interesting to me.

From an early age, I always loved things visual and mechanical. I loved to draw and play with Legos and my parents thought I would grow up to some kind of artist. However, I didn't really explore what to "be" until my 11th grade year. I discovered Robotics through BEST and that gave fuel to the light that had been sparked young. I was inspired by people like Kirk Sorensen who envision creating the energy future of tomorrow, now. Mechanical engineering felt like a natural choice after that.
